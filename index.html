↓   ABOUT   ↓

Hello! My name is Theresa, and this is a project created for FRS174: Drawing Data at Princeton University. The above desks were collected through self-submission via a Google form survey during the week of April 11th, 2021. 6 submissions of each gender were selected using a random number generator and redrawn in Adobe Illustrator.
